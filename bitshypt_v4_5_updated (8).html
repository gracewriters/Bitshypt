Five-layer VLOS enforcement & innovation

Every BitShypt drone enforces visual line of sight through five independent systems — including a revolutionary 5-light handoff protocol and VR headset operation that legally bypass traditional BVLOS restrictions. Any safety layer can trigger Return-to-Home. BitShypt doesn't just comply with drone laws — it reinvents how they work.

📍
Layer 1 — Distance Geofence

500m–1500m radius from operator GPS. Haversine check every 500ms. Configurable per jurisdiction (FAA Part 107 / EASA U-Space / CAAT). Warn margin at 450m.

🌤
Layer 2 — Weather Check

AWC METAR check every 60s. Blocks if visibility <3sm, wind >25kts, or flight category IFR/LIFR. TAF-based pre-mission go/no-go window calculation.

👁
Layer 3 — Computer Vision

MobileNetV3 on rear camera at 3fps. EMA α=0.3 over 9 frames (3s). NNAPI/Metal GPU acceleration. RTH triggers on 9 consecutive low-confidence frames.

🚦
Layer 4 — 5-Light Customer Handoff

On arrival, the drone flashes a unique 5-light repeating sequence — like Bitcoin's seed phrase verification. The customer confirms the sequence on their device, legally takes VLOS control, lands the drone, collects the package, and releases it back. Operator regains control once drone returns to sight range. No BVLOS waiver needed.
